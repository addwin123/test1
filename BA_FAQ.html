Placing and order

How do I redeem a discount or special offer?

Make sure that you're logged into your BonAppetite account on which you want to redeem the code.
1.Go to the Promo Code Redemption Page.
2.Enter your code in the box below Enter Your Code.
3.Click Redeem.

How can I ask for a refund?

Follow the instructions :
1. On your computer, go to BonAppetite.com/account.
2.Click Order History.
3. Find the order you want to return.
4. Select Request a refund or Report a problem and choose the option that describes your situation.
5. Complete the form and note that you'd like a refund.

How do I change my profile picture?

Change Profile Picture
1.Tap in the top right of BonAppetite, then tap your name.
2.Tap your profile picture.
3.Choose to Take Photo, Upload Photo, Add Frame or View Profile Picture. Tap Save.

Your acccount

I've forgotten my password.

Forgot Password
1. Visit Forgot Password.
2. Enter either the email address or username on the account.
3. Select Submit.
4. Check your inbox for a password reset email.
5. Click on the URL provided in the email and enter a new password.

How can I change my password?

Change your password
1. At your BonAppetite account, tap Security.
2. Under "Signing in to Bon Appetite," tap Password. You might need to sign in.
3. Enter your new password, then tap Change Password.

How to change my account name?

Manage your BonAppetite Account.
1.At the top, tap Personal info.
2.Under "Basic info," tap Name Edit. .
3.You may be asked to sign in.
4.Enter your name, then tap Done.
