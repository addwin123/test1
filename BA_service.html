Service
Here at Bon Appetit, we want to make sure you’re getting the best we have to give at all times. Our team specialises in providing Banquet French Service.

Live Streaming Kitchens service

A live stream of the kitchen proceedings assures customers of the hygiene compliance and sanitization standards that a restaurant is following. Live streaming the kitchen ensures that the current and prospective customers have full transparency and visibility on exactly what restaurants are doing to keep their teams, premises, and food 100% hygienic and safe.

Banquet French Service

Platters of foods are assembled in the kitchen. Servers take the platters to the table where guests are seated. The server, using two large silver forks in his or her serving hand places the food on the guests’ plates. Each food item is served by the server from platters to individual plates. Guests are served from the left.

Gueridon Service

This service is partially cooked food from the kitchen is taken to the Gueridon Trolly for cooking it completely. This partial cooking is done beside the guest table for achieving a particular appearance and aroma of food, and for exhibiting showmanship. It also offers a complete view of food. The waiter needs to perform the role of cook partially and needs to be dexterous.
